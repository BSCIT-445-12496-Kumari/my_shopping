Our Story

Founded in 2026, FreshMart started with a simple mission — make fresh, quality groceries accessible to every Indian household. What began as a small team of 5 people in Delhi has grown into a nationwide platform serving over half a million customers.

We partner directly with local farmers and producers, cutting out middlemen to bring you the freshest produce at the best prices. Every product on our platform is quality-checked before it reaches your door.
